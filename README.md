## Why I Built This

I spent 3 hours reading posts from traders who blew their accounts.

Every single one said the same thing: *"I knew I should stop. I just couldn't."*

Not a strategy problem. Not a knowledge problem. A biology problem.

Your brain registers a trading loss the same way it registers physical pain. The response? Fix it NOW. Not analyze. Not wait. Just — make it stop.

That's not weakness. That's being human.

The traders who survive aren't the ones with better strategies. They're the ones who built a wall between their emotions and their execution.

I built that wall. It's free. It's ugly. It works.

---

## What It Does

TiltCheck forces a pause before you trade when your mental state is compromised:

- **5-question readiness check** — scores your emotional state before you click "buy"
- **Forced cooldown** — if your score is low, you wait. No override. No negotiation.
- **Trade journal** — log what happened so you see the pattern, not just the loss
- **Session summary** — review your behavior, not just your P&L
- **Discipline streaks** — build momentum by showing up consistently

All data stays on your device. No cloud. No accounts. No tracking.

## Who Is This For?

- You who just reloaded your trading account for the third time this month
- You who journal every trade but still break your rules when tilted
- You who knows the math but can't feel it in the moment
- You who is tired of being your own worst enemy

If you've never felt the urge to "make it back" after a loss, this isn't for you.

## Installation

### Use the Web App

Visit **[tiltcheck.vercel.app](https://tiltcheck.vercel.app)** and install as a PWA:

- **Desktop** — Click the install icon in your browser's address bar
- **Mobile** — Share → Add to Home Screen

### Install the Chrome Extension

1. Clone the repo and open `chrome://extensions/`
2. Enable **Developer mode**
3. Click **Load unpacked** and select `tiltcheck-extension/`
4. An **Emergency Cool-Down** button appears on supported trading sites

### Build from Source
